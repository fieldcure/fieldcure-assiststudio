# Proposition

A 50-engineer SaaS company should mandate a 4-day work week (32 hours,
full pay) for all engineering teams.

## Background

The company is profitable and growing 20% year-over-year. It competes for
engineering talent against larger firms with bigger compensation budgets.
Engineering attrition over the last 12 months is 18%.

A recent employee survey identified burnout as the top concern, ahead of
compensation and career growth. Three competitors in adjacent markets have
adopted 4-day weeks in the past year. All three reported positive results
in their press releases.

The CFO is concerned about delivery velocity and on-call coverage. The CTO
is neutral. The CEO has asked the leadership team for a recommendation.

## Question

Should the company adopt the 4-day week as proposed?
